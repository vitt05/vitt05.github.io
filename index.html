Giving privacy without be private.

Tapanga is not about following people
It is about collaboration, share and building something together.

Nobody can follow you, see your history or comment in your photos.

It is really easy to use


You just need to label your photo with a combination of three elements:
Emoji, a hashtag name and location. It can be all three together, or just one.
The emoji place your photo in a global directory, everyone could see.
The hashtag name will send your photo to a wall, which is hidden from search, only those who know the exact name can see.
The location, only people who are around you will see.

Everyone wants to create a group of images that express who they are and what they want to be.
